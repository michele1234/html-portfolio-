Three top movies/ films

- Interstellar (2014);

In an unspecified future, drastic climate change is hitting agriculture hard. Maize is the only crop still able to grow, and a group of scientists is intent on crossing space to find new suitable places to grow it.

Characters:

  1. Joseph Cooper
  2. Professor Brand
  3. Matt Damon

- Fight Club (1999);

A Tyler Durden and a new friend vent their aggression by creating a fight club, which quickly takes on revolutionary connotations, eventually exposing Tyler Durden's true identity.

Characters:

  1. Tyler Durden
  2. Edward Norton

- The Social Network (2010);

A few years after creating Facebook in his Harvard dorm room, Mark Zuckerberg has become a billionaire, but his great success is leading to both personal and legal problems. By the way this film inspired me to go on and code.

Characters:
  1. Mark Zuckerberg
  2. Sean Parker

Three top tv shows(series)

- Suits (2011-2019);

Harvey Specter is one of New York's most prominent attorneys, cynical and ruthless, with a passion for tailored suits and social life. He has just become a senior partner in the law firm at which he works, Pearson Hardman, a role that forces him, despite himself, to take on a young associate: Harvey hates having to work in pairs, and he hates even more recent graduates fresh out of Harvard (Pearson Hardman accepts only those who graduated from Harvard). Mike Ross, on the other hand, is an extremely intelligent young man with a prodigious eidetic memory who never graduated from law school.

Characters:

  1. Harvey Specter
  2. Mike Ross
  3. Louis Litt

- The Playlist (2022);

The series centers on young Swedish tech entrepreneur Daniel Ek, then 23, and his key partners who revolutionized an entire industry by offering free and legal music streaming worldwide.

Characters:
  1. Daniel Ek
  2. Martin Lorenzon

- The Recuit (2022);

The show is about vicissitudes of Owen Hendricks, a young 24-year-old lawyer fresh out of law school who is hired as a lawyer for the CIA. Bullied by older colleagues, guilty of no longer loving their jobs, they hand him obnoxiously routine jobs that are discarded by all. Characters:


  1. Owen Hendrix
  2. Max Melaze
